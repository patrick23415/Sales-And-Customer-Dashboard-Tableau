# 📊 Sales & Customer Dashboard (Tableau)

This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing sales performance and customer behavior. It provides key business insights through KPI tracking, trend analysis, and customer segmentation.

This project was inspired by Data with Baraa and recreated independently as part of my data analytics portfolio.

---

## 🚀 Overview

The goal of this dashboard is to help stakeholders monitor business performance and answer key questions such as:

- How are sales, profit, and order volume changing over time?
- Which product categories are driving or losing profit?
- Who are the top-performing customers?
- How frequently do customers make purchases?
- How does performance vary by region and product category?

## 📈 Key Insights

- Sales, profit, and quantity all show strong year-over-year growth.
- A small group of customers contributes significantly to total profit.
- Most customers place a low number of orders, with fewer high-frequency buyers.
- Certain product subcategories generate consistent losses despite high sales.
- Sales trends reveal seasonal patterns and fluctuations throughout the year.
